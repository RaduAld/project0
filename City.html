My hometown is Bucharest, the capital of Romania. Bucharest is a beautiful city with amaizing buildings and gorgeous parks. I think that few people know how Bucharest really is, so here are 3 known places:
  • The Palace of the Parliment
    The Palace of the Parliment is located in the 5th sector of Bucharest, close to the Unirii Bulevard. It is the heaviest building in the world, weighting around 4,098,500,000 kilograms. Built over 13 years and finished in 1997, it was ordered by Nicolae Ceausescu, dictator of the Comunist Romania. Now the Palace houses the Senate, the Chamber of deputies, an international conference center along with 3 museums.
  • The Old Town
    Located in the center of Bucharest, the Old Town, known as the Old Center, it's all that was left after the Second World War. After 1989, along with many other places in Bucharest, it was changed beyond recocgnision, and at the moment it is popular throughout the world for its nightlife, with many restaurants, clubs and beautiful places.
  • Herăstrău Park
    The Herăstrău Park is located in the north of Bucharest, around The Herăstrău Lake. It is sectioned into two parts: a rustic and more natural one, where the Village Museum is located, a more peacefull area, and a more active one with many recreational activities, and some clubs.
